Prof. Dr. Surapaneni Krishna Mohan

Prof. Dr. Surapaneni Krishna Mohan is a renowned academic and medical educator with over 21 years of experience in teaching and research. Holding a PhD in Medical Biochemistry and several prestigious fellowships, he specializes in medical biochemistry, bioethics, clinical skills, and health professions education. He serves as an AHA-certified instructor for BLS and ACLS and holds leadership roles as Vice Principal and Head of multiple departments, including Medical Education, Bioethics, Clinical Skills & Simulation, and Research.

An accomplished researcher, Dr. Surapaneni has published 321 peer-reviewed papers indexed in Scopus and PubMed, contributing significantly to molecular virology, population health, and medical education. He also leads NABL-accredited laboratories and ethics committees, ensuring quality and innovation in healthcare training. For more, visit Google Scholar or ORCID.
